Here's what's gonna happen!

You're gonna input a rational non-negative number, then press 'Go!', then the program will count up to your number with the following rules:

  1. Starting at 1, the program will count up to your number by 1, displaying these numbers
  2. All numbers divisible by 15 will turn into "ping-pong"
  3. All numbers not divisible by 15, but divisible by 5 will be turned into "pong"
  4. All numbers not divisible by 15 or 5, but divisible by 3 will be turned into "ping"
  5. Feel free to play as many times as you would like!
